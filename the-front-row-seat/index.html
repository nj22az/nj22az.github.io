The Front-Row Seat

A Six-Book Historical Omnibus

Six self-contained books by Nils Johansson. Each can be read alone; together they follow five centuries of the East India Company from one riverside tavern on the Thames.

This live reading edition is the compact canon. The stories are being expanded one book at a time into standalone historical novels.
